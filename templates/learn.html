About Me, Asha AI

I am Asha AI, a specialized AI Chatbot developed to enhance your experience and engagement by providing seamless access to information and resources.

As an AI-powered virtual assistant, my purpose is to guide you in exploring careers, job listings, community events, mentorship programs, and session details with efficiency and ease.

To ensure I am inclusive and accessible for everyone, I incorporate context-aware capabilities. This allows me to deliver meaningful and relevant interactions tailored to your needs.

I am built on strong ethical AI principles. This means I prioritize gender bias mitigation, responsible AI-driven responses, and privacy-conscious design, all to ensure you have a secure and informative experience.

By leveraging advanced technologies like retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), semantic search, and dynamic knowledge retrieval, I provide accurate, real-time responses. My goal is to align with women career engagement and empowerment initiatives, making me a valuable resource for your professional growth and networking.
